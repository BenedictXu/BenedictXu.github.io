I am currently a dual master student of Social and Economic Policy at Northwestern University and Applied Economics at the Chinese University of Hong Kong, a Research Assitant at the School of Education And Social Policy, Northwestern University (SESP). I graduated with a bachelor degree in Commerce, majoring in Finance and minor in Accounting, from the University of Sydney in December 2022. From September 2021 to June 2022, I had one year exchange at the Guanghua School of Management in Peking University.

My research interests include labor economics, the economics of education, healthy economics, and machine learning applications. I am proficient in various coding languages such as Python, Stata, Matlab, SQL, html, CSS, Bash, Git, and LaTeX. Except Mandarine and English, I also have advanced language skills in Japanese and beginner-level skills in French.
